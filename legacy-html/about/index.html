About Giwa Investment

We are a Ghana-focused real estate team helping buyers, renters, and partners navigate property with patience and professionalism.

Giwa Investment works across residential sales, land, and rentals — including apartments and houses. Our clients range from first-time renters to families buying a home and investors exploring aligned opportunities.

We believe in plain language, honest availability, and respect for your time. When you work with us, you get responsive communication (including WhatsApp), organized viewings where possible, and no hype about titles or returns — those conversations belong with qualified legal and financial advisers.

Service areas

We support clients across Ghana; tell us your preferred region and budget and we will be direct about how we can help.
